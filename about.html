Striving for the ideal

WIZARD - a reliable supplier of equipment for engineering systems of water supply, drainage, heating. For 20 years, our activities have been aimed at ensuring long-term, mutually beneficial and effective cooperation, high quality standards and an impeccable brand reputation.

The company's product portfolio includes more than 30 officially imported brands, more than 50,000 products from the world's leading manufacturers, many of which are recognized leaders in their industries.

A separate area of ​​activity of the WIZARD brand is the design, equipment and installation of engineering systems for residential and industrial infrastructure. Among the projects implemented by WIZARD - different in complexity of work: from airports and stadiums to shops and private homes.

Our company pays special attention to serving its partners. To increase the efficiency of cooperation, we have developed a multifunctional Personal Account of the WIZARD partner. It allows you to instantly order goods, update their availability, compare features, monitor the status of your online order and accurately calculate your logistics processes.

We train employees and colleagues

We conduct seminars and trainings for managers, technicians and staff. We develop systems of motivation and support. We have developed and regularly conduct activities to check the quality of service and staff training of our partners, as well as launched the WIZARD Sales School project.

We have created an online platform to work with distributors so that the purchase process does not take time, is personal and secure, and all the data is always at hand.

Ліга WAKO WIZARD

Особливу увагу ми приділяємо спортивному вихованню дітей та юнацтва, тому що вважаємо це фундаментом здорової і щасливої нації. Школа продажів

Компанія WIZARD дотримується людиноорієнтованого підходу в роботі та навчанні, цінує проактивних і ефективних співробітників. Саме тому в компанії є можливість безкоштовного навчання для всіх співробітників.
